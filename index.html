About DingTai Auto

Shenzhen Dingtai Automobile Service Co., Ltd. (with its wholly-owned subsidiary: Hong Kong Jinghong Technology Co., Ltd.) has 15 years of experience in automobile sales. Our market experience and industry insight enable us to effectively meet customers' needs.

We specialize in providing comprehensive automobile options covering both Chinese and global brands. Through close cooperation with leading domestic and international brands, we offer customers a diverse range of choices.

Our business is built on the sales of new cars, used cars, and New Energy Vehicles. Meanwhile, we provide customers with flexible purchasing solutions and all-round services, including global logistics and after-sales support.

As the market evolves, we leverage technological innovation and service upgrades to deliver more personalized and efficient solutions to our customers. Our rapid development in overseas markets aims to utilize our brand strength and expertise to serve more overseas clients.
